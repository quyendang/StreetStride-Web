Privacy Policy

Clear, local-first privacy information.

Last updated: June 19, 2026. StreetStride is designed as an iPhone treadmill companion without a StreetStride account system.

1. Overview

StreetStride helps you plan treadmill routes and view Google Street View imagery while you exercise. This policy explains what information the app handles and how it is used.

We do not operate a StreetStride account system and we do not run a StreetStride backend that stores your run history, route history, health data, or precise location data.

2. Information StreetStride handles

  • Route and location information such as selected start points, destination points, waypoints, route distance, and route progress.
  • Run information such as duration, distance, speed, completion state, achievements, goals, and history saved on your device.
  • App preferences such as unit settings, audio choices, theme preferences, and cache controls.
  • Optional photos or share cards only when you choose to save or share them.

3. Google and Apple services

StreetStride uses Google Maps Platform services, including route and Street View functionality. When you create or preview routes, relevant location, route, device, network, and request information may be sent to Google and handled under Google's terms and privacy policy.

StreetStride may also use Apple services and frameworks such as App Store, StoreKit, HealthKit, Keychain, Photos, and system permission prompts. Apple's handling of information is governed by Apple's privacy practices and your device settings.

4. Your choices and permissions

  • You can manage Location permission in iOS Settings.
  • You can manage Health permission in the Health app and iOS Settings.
  • You can manage Photos permission in iOS Settings.
  • You can delete local app data by removing the app from your device.

5. Children's privacy

StreetStride is not directed to children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, contact us.

6. Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to support@streetstride.app.
