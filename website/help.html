Help center

Short answers before your next run.

Start with the common tasks below. For account, billing, or device-specific issues, contact support.

01

Plan a route

Search for places, choose start and destination points, then review distance and route readiness before continuing.

02

Start a run

Pick a treadmill pace, confirm the route preview, and begin the countdown when you are ready to move.

03

Fix route loading

Try nearby roads, shorter routes, or a stronger connection. Some areas have limited Street View coverage.

04

Manage permissions

Use iOS Settings to change Location, Health, Photos, or notification access at any time.

05

Track progress

Open Profile to review streaks, weekly goals, achievements, and saved routes from your local app experience.

06

Share a summary

After a run, use the summary screen to save or share a card only when you choose to do so.

Still stuck?

Send support the route, device model, iOS version, and a short description.
